Rower Stuff

A range of useful calculators and tools for rowers, coaches and cross fit athletes on Android.

About

This is a simple mobile app for android that includes a variety of tools and calculators for rowers and rowing coaches. This app allows quick and easy access to vital calculations that the athletes use to analyse the data from their training. It also keeps all the calculations together whereas before you had to remember multiple different websites for different calculations.

Features Included:

- Pace calculator

- Watt calculator

- Weight adjustment calculator

- Prediction calculator

- VO2 max estimate

- Rate Tool

- Percentage pace/watts calculator

- Steady State Tool

Quality of Life Features:

- Easily clear calculators to make moving between calculations easier

- Easily copy data from each calculation right to you clipboard

- Dark mode

Dev Cycle

This app began development in 2018, were work was done on and off on a simple version of the app with only 3 features. I then took a break due to exams, but then started the version 2 redesign in the summer of 2019. The version 2 redesign made the app much easier to navigate, look better and also added new features. I have then gradually been adding new features and fixing small bugs since the release. It now has 7 important tools and a host of quality of life features like easy data copying and clearing.
