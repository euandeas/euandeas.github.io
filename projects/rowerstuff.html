About

This is a simple mobile app for android that includes a variety of tools and calculators for rowers and rowing coaches. This app allows quick and easy access to vital calculations that the athletes use to analyse the data from their training. It also keeps all the calculations together whereas before you had to remember multiple different websites for different calculations.

Dev Cycle

This app began in 2018, were I started work on and off work on a simple version of the app with only 3 features. I then had to take a break for a while due to exams, but then started the version 2 redesign in the summer of 2019. The version 2 redesign made the app much easier to navigate, look better and also added new features. I have then gradually been adding new features and fixing small bugs since the release. It now has 7 important tools and a host of quality of life features like easy data copying and clearing.

Technologies Used

This app was built using C# and Xamarin.Android
